Childhood

I was born and raised in Toronto, Canada to immigrant Bangladeshi Muslim parents. Since childhood till now, I've lived in the Alexandra Park community in downtown Toronto near famous city landmarks like Kensington Market, Chinatown and Queen West.

The Alexandra Park neighbourhood is a historic neighbourhood of Toronto that is known to for its immigrant roots starting way back in the early 20th century.

Sports

Growing up in Alexandra Park, I grew up surrounded by sports facilities and athletes. Within less than a block from my house there's a basketball court, a hockey rink, a skate park, a soccer field and 2 community centres. Naturally, I also got into sports and mainly played hockey as well as basketball and a bit of soccer.

Games

Besides sports, I was also heavily invested in video games.Some of my favourite games were Team Fortress 2, Mario Galaxy and Super Smash Ultimate. Though recently, I don't play as much now besides the occassional FIFA game with friends.

Studies

As of now, I spend most of my time on school work and making projects to eventually land a co-op.
